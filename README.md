# Privacy Policy for E-Task

**Last Updated:** November 26, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to E-Task ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you register for E-Task, we collect:
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, password (encrypted)
- **Profile Information:** Profile picture, job title, organization name
- **Contact Information:** Email address for notifications and support

### Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Log Data:** IP address, browser type, access times, pages viewed
- **App Usage:** Features used, actions performed, performance data
- **Location Data:** General location based on IP address (not precise GPS)

### Content You Create
- **Projects and Tasks:** Titles, descriptions, due dates, priorities
- **Comments and Messages:** Team communications and task comments
- **Files:** Attachments uploaded to tasks and projects
- **Activity History:** Changes made to projects and tasks

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

### Provide Services
- Create and manage your account
- Enable project and task management features
- Facilitate team collaboration and communication
- Sync data across your devices
- Send notifications about task updates and mentions

### Improve Services
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Monitor and analyze trends and usage
- Develop new features and functionality
- Troubleshoot technical issues

### Communication
- Send service-related emails (account verification, password reset)
- Send notifications about tasks, projects, and team activity
- Respond to your support requests
- Send updates about new features (with your consent)

### Security and Legal
- Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
- Comply with legal obligations
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect our rights and property

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT sell your personal data.

We may share your information with:

### Team Members
- Information you share in projects is visible to team members with access
- Your profile information is visible to your workspace members

### Service Providers
We use third-party services that may access your data:
- **Cloud Hosting:** AWS/Google Cloud for data storage
- **Analytics:** Firebase Analytics for app usage insights
- **Authentication:** Google Sign-In for account creation
- **Email Services:** For sending notifications and support emails
- **Crash Reporting:** Firebase Crashlytics for error tracking

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:
- Court orders or legal processes
- Government requests
- Protection of our rights or safety of users

### Business Transfers
If E-Task is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption:** Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- **Authentication:** Secure password hashing and token-based authentication
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access to limit data exposure
- **Regular Backups:** Automated backups to prevent data loss
- **Security Monitoring:** Continuous monitoring for suspicious activity

### Data Retention
- **Active Accounts:** We retain your data while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Backups:** Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days
- **Legal Requirements:** Some data may be retained longer if required by law

### International Data Transfers
Your data may be transferred to and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Export:** Download your data in a portable format
- **Restriction:** Limit how we process your data

### Privacy Settings
You can control:
- **Notifications:** Enable/disable push notifications in app settings
- **Profile Visibility:** Control what information is visible to team members
- **Email Preferences:** Manage email notification preferences

### Account Deletion
To delete your account:
1. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
2. Confirm deletion
3. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Contact us at support@etask.com for assistance with data requests.

## Children's Privacy

E-Task is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

- Improve user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect app functionality.

- Right to access your personal data

- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@etask.com.

- Right to know what personal information is collected
